My works

RLCTFv1 Compitition

I supervised with my team R-LORDS the RLCTF competition that was held at the University of Science and Technology. The competition included a variety of questions, and I wrote several diverse challenges, including Reverse Engineering, Crypto, Steganography, and OSINT. The event served as both an educational experience for beginner students and a competitive challenge for advanced students.

CTF course

Successfully organized a CTF Introduction Course, providing junior students with hands-on experience in cybersecurity fundamentals, including cryptography, web security, reverse engineering, Web exploitation and digital forensics. The seminar featured interactive challenges and practical exercises, enhancing participants' problem-solving skills and understanding of CTF competitions.

Modifyte Tool

Modifyte Tool is a dynamic file analysis application that efficiently extracts, modifies, and analyzes file content. It offers intuitive text viewing, extraction, and editing features while providing detailed file metrics. Ideal for developers, security researchers, and data analysts, it brings a professional touch to file management.
